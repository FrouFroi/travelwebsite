From Peaks to Hidden Retreats!

Adventure and relaxation await in Cavite’s stunning landscapes!

Begin your journey with a trek to Mt. Palay-Palay, home to Pico de Loro, where the iconic monolith and sweeping views await adventurers.

Unwind at Tagaytay Highlands, a luxurious retreat with cool mountain air, scenic vistas, and relaxing amenities.

Finally, escape to Ternate Beach, a tranquil seaside spot surrounded by rugged cliffs and pristine waters, offering a peaceful break from the city’s hustle.

Hidden Gems of Tagaytay

Discover secret waterfalls and hidden cafes for yourself and love ones.

Spend your day exploring scenic parks like People’s Park in the Sky, Crosswinds, and Picnic Grove, where nature and relaxation blend perfectly.

Delight in charming hidden cafes like Alitaptap Cafe, SSPACE, and Barista Loft perfect for sharing meaningful moments with your loved ones.

Capture stunning photos or share heartfelt conversations while surrounded by picturesque views and cool breezes. These beautiful spots offer the perfect backdrop for meaningful moments and cherished memories.

Take A Dive Deep in the Jungle

Discover the hidden beauty of nature waiting to be explored.

In Cavite, you can visit the enchanting Balite Falls in Amadeo, the serene Malibiclibic Falls in Gen.Aguinaldo, or the picturesque Cabag Cave and Waterfalls in Maragondon.

These stunning spots promise a refreshing escape and a chance to reconnect with the wild wonders of the province

Each destination has been carefully selected to provide an authentic experience of Cavite's and natural beauty.
