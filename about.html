Meet The Web Developer

Jose Nomel Aventurado

Lakwatsero & Web Developer

Driven by his adventurous spirit, Nomel, a proud "lakwatsero," roamed far and wide to capture the essence of Cavite's natural and cultural beauty

His journey began in childhood, being born and raised in the vibrant province of Cavite. Growing up surrounded by its lush landscapes, historic landmarks, and rich traditions, Nomel developed a deep appreciation for the place he called home.

His travels were not just about the sights but also about immersing himself in the vibrant culture, savoring local delicacies, and connecting with the warm-hearted people of Cavite.

Through his explorations, Nomel sought to preserve and share the stories of Cavite's rich heritage, showcasing its unique charm and inspiring others to experience its wonders firsthand.
